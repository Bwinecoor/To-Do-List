    How To

    In order to use the to do list you must first click on the white box with the word "Title" on it. Next, Type in whatever it is you need to do. Lastly, click add and whatever you need to do will appear on the list.

    Click on the task if you completed it and a checkmark will appear. Click on the X on the right side to get rid of the task.

    What is a To-Do List?

    A to do list a list of things that you need to do. They are mainly used by people who have a difficult time remembering things and use it to keep track of what they need to do.
